Where did we go?

Locations

To distribute surveys, we chose locations that were accessible to all New York City residents: Prospect Park and Atlantic Terminal. The Long Meadow, the Lake, the Carousel, and the park's perimeter are some the attractions we visited. At Altantic Terminal, we visited the downtown R, N, & D trains platform, the Long Island Railroad, and the station's tunnel.
