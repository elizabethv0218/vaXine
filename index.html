Quality of Life Issue: Vaccination Exemptions in NYC

The spread of misinformation about vaccines is high and it can convince others to not get vaccinated. Vaccine exemptions are a problem to not just the individual exempting, but also those who interact with the individual. It is our goal to educate students and parents about vaccinations in order to help them make educated choices concerning their health.

What are vaccines?

A vaccine introduces the human body to a dead or weakened version of a virus, so when it encounters the virus again the immune system knows how to combat it.

How are they beneficial?

When people get a vaccine, they prepare their body to defend itself against a disease. Additionally, they are doing everyone else a favor by helping decrease the chances of spreading a disease. The more people vaccinate, the more it will help improve personal and public health.
